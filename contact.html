About This Site

FreelanceIncomeCalc.com is a free, browser-based gross income calculator for freelancers. It converts hourly rates to annual, monthly, weekly, and estimated quarterly gross figures. All calculations run locally in your browser. No account is required, and no input data is stored or transmitted.

This site is part of a network of free financial utility tools. See the Legal and Privacy pages for full terms and data practices.
