About This Calculator

How FreelanceIncomeCalc.com works and what it estimates.

What It Does

FreelanceIncomeCalc.com is a browser-based calculator that estimates gross freelance income across hourly, weekly, monthly, and annual time periods based on your hourly rate and working schedule.

How It Works

Enter your hourly rate (or annual gross target), hours per week, and weeks per year. The calculator multiplies these inputs to derive your estimated gross income. All calculations run locally in your browser — no data is sent anywhere.

What It Does Not Do

This tool provides gross revenue estimates only. It does not model taxes, deductions, business expenses, benefits, or any other adjustments. For tax and financial planning, consult a qualified professional.

About the Network

FreelanceIncomeCalc.com is part of the Calc-HQ financial calculator network — a collection of free, browser-based financial tools.
