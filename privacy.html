Overview

FreelanceIncomeCalc.com is a static, browser-based tool. We do not operate user accounts, do not collect personal information, and do not transmit calculator inputs to any server. This policy describes what limited data interactions occur when you use this site.

Calculator Data

All calculations on this site run entirely within your browser using local JavaScript. No input you enter into the calculator is transmitted to our servers or any third party. We do not store, log, or process any calculation data.

Cookies and Third-Party Advertising

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to this site and other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. You can also opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

We do not control the cookies placed by Google AdSense. Google's privacy policy governs its data collection and use practices.

Server Logs

Standard web server access logs may record your IP address, browser type, referring URL, and pages visited, as is common with any website. These logs are used solely for security and operational purposes and are not shared with third parties for marketing.

No Data Sales

We do not sell personal information. We do not have personal information to sell — the calculator collects none.

This site contains links to other websites, including related financial tools. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. Review their privacy policies independently.
